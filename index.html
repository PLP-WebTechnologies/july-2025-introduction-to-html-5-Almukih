About Us

Welcome to Alfred's Haven, where every dish tells a story and every guest is treated like family. Nestled in the heart of our community, Alfred's Haven was founded with one mission: to create a warm and inviting space where great food and memorable moments come together. We specialize in serving delicious, freshly-prepared meals inspired by local flavors and global favorites. Whether you're here for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a celebration with loved ones, we strive to make your experience unforgettable. At Alfred's Haven, it's not just about food, it's about feeling at home.

Starters

  1. Samosas
    • Stuffed with beef, chiken, or vegetables (Your choice).


  2. Soup of the day.
    • options like beef broth, chicken soup, or creamy vegetables.

  3. Chapati Rolls.
    • Chapati filled with minced meat or veggies and rolled.

  4. Grilled Mshikaki.
    • Skewered and Grilled marinated meat cubes.

  5. Mandazi with Coconut Dip.
    • Lightly spiced fried dough, served with sweet or savory dip.

Main Courses

  1. Nyama Choma (Grilled Meat)
    • Charcoal-grilled beef, goat, or chicken, served with Kachumbari and Ugali

  2. Beef Stew (Nyama ya Mchuzi)
    • Slow-cooked beef in a rich tomato-onion sauce, served with rice, chapati or ugali

  3. Wet or Dry-fried Goat (Mbuzi)
    • Tender goat cooked with spices and herbs, served with your preferred starch

  4. Fried Chicked (Kuku Fry)
    • Deep-fried or pan-fried chicken pieces, spiced and served with chips or rice

  5. Chicken stew (Kuku wa kupika)
    • Traditional Kenyan chicken stew in thick tomato gravy

Desserts

  1. Mandazi
    • Served with honey or a dusting of powdered sugar

  2. Kaimati
    • Crispy outside, soft inside

  3. Sweet Ugali bites.
    • Ugali mixed with sugar and milk, served as the warm bite-sized treats.
